About

Welcome to Portland's inaugural premier tennis tournament- an exciting new tradition designed to showcase our most competitive players. This event will bring together athletes from across the region to contend in a professionally organized event.

Beyond the court, this tournament is a celebration for the entire community. Spectators are invited to watch tennis of an elite level up close and participate in a fun atmosphere.

This tournament is made possible by the support of our sponsors, with special thanks to our title sponsor, Player's Racquet Shop.
