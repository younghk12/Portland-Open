Tournament Format and Registration

This is a prize money event, so to ensure the highest quality, the singles divisions will be using an acceptance list, which orders our registrants by UTR. In singles, the top 24 highest rated players will be directly accepted in the main draw, and the rest will be placed into a qualifying draw where the cap will be 32 players. All divisions will be single elimination.

Players may sign up for multiple divisions, but please note there will be no discounts if you sign up for more than 1 event. For doubles, one of the players must sign up for you and your partner, and must also pay for your team's entry, which is $100. If you're the one signing up we trust you can figure out a way to have your partner pay you back!

No refund once the registration deadline has passed!

Please contact the tournament organizer with any questions or concerns.
