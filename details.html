Tournament Details

  • Lewis and Clark College
  • Jun 29 - Jul 5
  • $75-113.13
  • Singles / Doubles

Tournament Format and Registration

The singles divisions will be using an acceptance list, which orders our registrants by UTR. In singles, the top 24 highest rated players will be directly accepted in the main draw, and the rest will be placed into a qualifying draw where the cap will be 32 players.

For doubles, one of the players must sign up for you and your partner, and must also pay for your team's entry, which is $100. If you're the one signing up we trust you can figure out a way to have your partner pay you back!

Players may sign up for multiple divisions, but please note there will be no discounts if you sign up for more than 1 event. No refund once the registration deadline has passed!

All divisions will be single elimination.

The tournament schedule will be made available in the days leading up to the event. Tournament draws will be available shortly after the registration deadline of June 25th. All information is also available through the tournament registration via UTR.

Please contact the tournament organizer with any questions or concerns.

Match Details

Court: Bubble Hardcourt

Warm-up: All players will receive a 7-minute warm-up before each match.

Format: All matches will be 2 out of 3 sets with regular scoring.

Balls: Wilson US Open Extra Duty

In order to ensure fair and equal play, tournament officials will be present on site. Chair Umpires will be present for all semifinal and final matches.

The Portland Open is a prize money event. All prize money is made possible by our sponsors. Prize money will be awarded in all divisions. Prize money will be determined by the number of entrants within each division. Prize money breakdown will be available June 15th.
